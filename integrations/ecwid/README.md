Update your .env with your AIRTABLE and ECWID connection information.

Generate an ECWID token:

* Log in to your Ecwid Admin Panel: Access your store's backend using your credentials.
* Navigate to the Apps Section: Go to the "Apps" or "Develop Apps" section within your admin panel.
* Create a New Custom App: Click on the option to create a new custom application.
* Configure App Permissions: Assign the necessary permissions to your app based on the data you need to access or modify through the API (e.g., read products, update orders, manage customers).
* Access App Details: After creating the app, go to its details page.
* Reveal and Copy Tokens: On the app details page, you will find options to "Show secret token" and view the public token. Copy the relevant token(s) for your integration.
